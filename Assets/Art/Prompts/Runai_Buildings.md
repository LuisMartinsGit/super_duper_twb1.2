# Runai Building Prompts

> **Culture Identity:** Nomadic traders. Architecture is Arabic-inspired — flowing curves, horseshoe arches, ornate geometric tile work, open-air courtyards, domed roofs, wind towers, and sun-bleached sandstone. Colors: cyan fabric/banners/awnings, sandstone walls.

---

## Era 1 — Universal Buildings (Runai Style)

### Hall
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A grand sandstone palace serving as the faction's main hall. Warm tan sandstone walls with intricate carved geometric lattice patterns. A large central dome of hammered copper patinated to teal, flanked by two smaller domes. A wide horseshoe arch entrance with alternating cream and terracotta voussoirs. Slender decorative minarets at each corner with pointed copper finials. An open-air inner courtyard glimpsed through the archway with a tiled fountain. Cyan silk banners drape from the upper walls. Carved wooden mashrabiya screens cover the upper windows. Ornamental crenellations along the roofline in a repeating geometric pattern. Potted desert palms flank the entrance. The structure conveys prosperity, elegance, and trade wealth. Fantasy medieval Arabic palace style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Hut
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A small cubic sandstone dwelling for housing workers. Sun-bleached tan mud-brick walls with rounded edges and a flat roof edged with a low decorative parapet of stepped merlons. A single horseshoe arch doorway with a cyan fabric curtain draped across it. One arched window with a carved wooden mashrabiya screen. A rooftop wind catcher tower for ventilation rising from one corner. A small cyan pennant on a thin pole. Clay pots and a woven basket sit beside the entrance. Simple but graceful, reflecting Runai craftsmanship even in humble dwellings. Fantasy medieval Arabic desert dwelling style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Gatherers Hut
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A sandstone storehouse and resource depot styled as a desert trading post. Warm tan mud-brick walls with a flat roof and a shaded colonnade of horseshoe arches along the front. Wide open archway entrance draped with cyan and sandstone-striped canvas awnings for shade. Wooden crates, clay amphorae, and woven sacks of goods stacked under the colonnade. A hanging bronze scale near the entrance. Carved geometric border motifs run along the upper walls. A rooftop wind catcher for cooling the stored goods. A wooden sign with a crescent and scales emblem. Terracotta tile detailing along the base. Fantasy medieval Arabic caravanserai storehouse style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Barracks
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A sandstone military training compound with an open drill courtyard. Tall tan sandstone walls with decorative carved arabesques and arched crenellations along the parapet. A horseshoe arch entrance reinforced with bronze-studded cedar doors. An open courtyard with sand-covered training ground, weapon racks holding curved scimitars and round shields. A shaded arcade of pointed arches along the inner walls where soldiers rest. A square lookout tower rises from one corner with a cyan banner bearing a crossed-scimitar emblem. Bronze lanterns hang from iron hooks on the exterior walls. Geometric tile mosaics in cyan and terracotta decorate the entrance arch. Fantasy medieval Arabic fortress barracks style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Era 1 — Choice Buildings (Runai Style)

### Temple of Ridan
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A sandstone mosque-temple dedicated to the god Ridan. Graceful tan sandstone walls with bands of intricate geometric carved relief. A grand central dome of burnished copper with a golden crescent-star finial of Ridan at its apex. A tall slender minaret rises beside the main dome, ringed with delicate balconies and capped with a smaller dome. A monumental horseshoe arch entrance framed with alternating cream and lapis-blue voussoirs. Stained glass in geometric star patterns fills the arched windows, casting colored light. Cyan ceremonial banners cascade down the facade. An interior courtyard with an ablution fountain is visible through the doorway. Muqarnas honeycomb vaulting decorates the entrance alcove. An atmosphere of serene devotion and beauty. Fantasy medieval Arabic sacred mosque style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Vault of Almierra
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A squat, heavily fortified sandstone treasury vault. Thick tan sandstone block walls with almost no openings — only narrow ventilation slits near the flat roofline. A single massive bronze door with intricate geometric engravings and a heavy ring-pull lock mechanism. Defensive stepped merlons along the rooftop. Corner buttresses with carved lion-head reliefs symbolizing guardianship. A band of repeating interlocking star tile mosaic in cyan and gold runs around the upper wall perimeter. Subtle gold leaf accents on the door frame suggesting immense wealth within. Small cyan flags on short poles at each corner. Sand-colored plaster over the lower walls, raw cut stone above. Fantasy medieval Arabic fortified treasury style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Fiendstone Keep
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A dark imposing sandstone fortress radiating menace. Built from near-black fiendstone blocks with a faint reddish-purple mineral veining. Pointed horseshoe arches twisted into aggressive angular shapes. A central dome of blackened copper with jagged spike finials. Corner towers with onion-shaped caps ending in iron barbs. Narrow windows glow with a dim ember-orange inner light. The entrance arch is framed by carved serpentine motifs and snarling face reliefs. Dark iron chains and hooked brackets adorn the walls. Faint dark smoke curls from roof vents. Geometric patterns on the walls are distorted and angular, corrupting the Runai aesthetic. Cyan banners replaced with charcoal pennants bearing a jagged fist emblem. Fantasy medieval Arabic dark citadel style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Era 2 — Runai Culture Buildings

### Outpost
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A small fortified desert waystation serving as a Runai trade node and forward outpost. A cylindrical sandstone watchtower with a domed copper roof and an open observation balcony ringed with arched openings. A low surrounding wall of tan mud-brick forms a small courtyard with a watering trough and hitching posts for camels. A horseshoe arch gate with a cyan fabric canopy overhead. Signal fire brazier on the tower top. A carved stone waymarker with directional trade route arrows stands beside the gate. Cyan pennant flying from the dome finial. Narrow arrow slits in the tower walls. A small rooftop wind catcher. The outpost feels like a remote but well-maintained caravan stop. Fantasy medieval Arabic desert watchtower style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Trade Hub
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A large ornate sandstone trading house where caravans are dispatched and trade routes managed. Warm tan sandstone with richly carved geometric facades. A wide central courtyard visible through a grand horseshoe arch entrance, with a decorative mosaic tile floor in cyan, terracotta, and cream. Multiple arched alcoves along the interior walls serve as merchant stalls and counting houses. A tall central tower with an open-arched belfry for signaling caravans, topped with a copper dome and cyan pennant. A loading dock on one side with wooden cranes and rope pulleys for moving trade goods. Stacked crates, rolled carpets, and clay jars crowd the courtyard edges. Bronze hanging lanterns illuminate the archways. Cyan and gold striped awnings shade the stall openings. Geometric star-pattern tile bands decorate all upper walls. Fantasy medieval Arabic grand caravanserai style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Trading Post
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A modest sandstone waypoint building that forms a node in the Runai trade chain network. A compact square structure of warm tan mud-brick with a flat roof and decorative stepped merlons. A single horseshoe arch entrance with a cyan-striped awning. A covered stall area extending from one wall with a wooden counter, a hanging bronze scale, and shelved goods. A carved stone trade route marker with the post number engraved stands prominently beside the door. A small stable lean-to on one side for caravan animals. Stacked trade goods — bundled fabrics, sealed clay amphorae, spice sacks — sit under the awning. A slender signal pole with a cyan flag. Geometric tile trim in cyan around the doorway arch. Compact, functional, and built for the trade network. Fantasy medieval Arabic desert trading waystation style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Thessara's Bazaar
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A magnificent sprawling sandstone bazaar complex — the Runai mobile headquarters and crown jewel. A massive central structure with a grand ribbed dome of burnished copper crowned with a golden crescent finial. Four ornate corner towers with onion-shaped copper domes and open balconies. A monumental horseshoe arch entrance flanked by carved stone columns with spiraling geometric patterns. The interior is a vaulted market hall — rows of merchant stalls, hanging lanterns, draped fabrics, and piled trade goods visible through the entrance. An upper gallery with carved mashrabiya screens overlooks the market floor. Cyan, gold, and crimson silk banners cascade from every tower and parapet. A raised platform with a throne-like merchant's seat serves as the command center. Fountains and potted palms in the forecourt. Intricate mosaic tile floors in star-and-polygon patterns. Muqarnas vaulting in the entrance alcove. The structure radiates opulence, trade dominance, and cultural pride. Fantasy medieval Arabic grand bazaar palace style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Siege Workshop
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A large open-air sandstone workshop compound for constructing siege weapons. Tan sandstone perimeter walls with arched openings instead of solid gates, allowing large equipment to be moved freely. A partially assembled sand ballista — a large crossbow-like siege weapon on a wheeled wooden frame — sits in the central yard. Stockpiles of shaped timber, coiled ropes, bronze fittings, and iron bolts organized under shaded lean-to canopies. A covered smithing area with a clay forge, bellows, and anvil for metalwork. A roofed carpentry section with workbenches, saws, and chisels. A wooden crane with a counterweight for lifting heavy beams. Sand and wood shavings cover the packed-earth floor. Cyan engineer's pennant with a ballista emblem on a pole. Geometric carved border along the upper wall. Fantasy medieval Arabic military workshop style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Style Notes

- **Stone color:** Sun-bleached tan sandstone (#C4A872) as primary, warm cream (#E8D8B8) for accents and voussoirs
- **Fabric/banners:** Cyan (#40BFD0) — Runai's primary culture color
- **Metal:** Bronze, hammered copper (patinated teal-green on domes), occasional gold leaf trim
- **Roofs:** Copper domes, flat rooftops with stepped merlons, wind catchers
- **Shared motifs:** Horseshoe arches, geometric tile mosaics, mashrabiya screens, wind catchers, stepped merlons, star-and-polygon patterns
- **Scale hierarchy:** Thessara's Bazaar > Hall/Trade Hub > Barracks/Siege Workshop > Outpost/Trading Post/Gatherers Hut > Hut
- **Lighting:** Warm golden desert light, bronze lantern glow, teal copper reflections
