# Alanthor Building Prompts

> **Culture Identity:** Industrial forgemasters. Architecture is rich, robust, square, and inspired by European castles. Heavy stone masonry, crenellated battlements, iron reinforcements, heraldic banners. Colors: sage green fabric/banners, warm grey stone.

---

## Era 1 — Universal Buildings (Alanthor Style)

### Hall
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A grand square stone keep serving as the faction's main hall. Thick ashlar walls with a crenellated parapet and corner turrets capped with pointed slate roofs. A wide arched gatehouse entrance reinforced with iron portcullis. Sage green heraldic banners hang from the upper walls. A central courtyard is visible through the open gate. Warm grey limestone construction with darker grey foundation stones. Iron-banded wooden doors. Chimney stacks emit faint smoke. The structure conveys wealth, authority, and permanence. Fantasy medieval European castle style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Hut
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A small but sturdy square stone dwelling for housing workers. Thick mortared walls of warm grey limestone blocks with a steep slate hip roof. A single arched wooden door with iron strap hinges. One narrow arched window with wooden shutters. A short stone chimney on the roof. A small sage green pennant on a pole at one corner. Simple but well-built, reflecting Alanthor's robust masonry even in modest housing. Fantasy medieval European cottage-keep style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Gatherers Hut
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A square stone storehouse and resource depot. Robust warm grey limestone walls with a flat crenellated roofline, resembling a miniature fortified warehouse. An open arched entrance wide enough for carts. Wooden shelving and crates visible inside through the archway. Iron wall-mounted torch sconces on the exterior. A hanging wooden sign with a pickaxe and wheat sheaf emblem. A small crane arm with rope and pulley extends from the upper wall for hoisting goods. Sage green canvas awning over the entrance. Fantasy medieval European fortified storehouse style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Barracks
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A rectangular fortified stone barracks with a training yard. Tall robust walls of warm grey limestone with arrow-slit windows and a crenellated parapet walkway. A strong arched entrance with an iron-studded oak door. An attached open-air courtyard flanked by a covered colonnade with weapon racks and training dummies visible. A square watchtower rises from one corner with a sage green banner bearing a sword emblem. Iron torch brackets line the outer walls. Stone steps lead up to the parapet. Fantasy medieval European castle barracks style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Era 1 — Choice Buildings (Alanthor Style)

### Temple of Ridan
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A square fortified stone cathedral-temple dedicated to the god Ridan. Massive buttressed walls of warm grey limestone rising to a steep gabled roof of dark slate. A grand rose window of stained glass dominates the front facade above an ornate arched entrance flanked by carved stone pillars. A square bell tower rises from one side, capped with a pointed spire and a glowing golden symbol of Ridan. Sage green ceremonial banners drape from the upper walls. Interior candlelight glows warmly through the windows. Gargoyle waterspouts at the roof corners. An atmosphere of reverent strength. Fantasy medieval European fortified cathedral style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Vault of Almierra
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A squat, immensely thick-walled square stone vault for storing faction wealth. Oversized warm grey limestone blocks with visible iron clamps holding them together. A single heavy iron vault door with multiple locks and a wheel mechanism as the only entrance. No windows — only narrow ventilation slits near the roofline. The flat roof is edged with low crenellations and features a large iron-bound chest emblem in relief on the front wall. Corner buttresses reinforce the structure. Sage green flags on short poles at each corner. Subtle golden trim around the door frame suggesting the riches within. Fantasy medieval European fortified treasury style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Fiendstone Keep
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A dark imposing square stone keep radiating menace and power. Built from near-black fiendstone blocks with a faint reddish-purple veining. Tall angular walls with aggressive crenellations shaped like fangs. Four corner towers with conical roofs topped with iron spike finials. Narrow windows glow with a dim ember-orange inner light. A heavy portcullis entrance framed by carved grotesque faces in the stone. Dark iron chains and hooks adorn the walls as decoration. Faint wisps of dark smoke curl from vents. The structure feels oppressive and warlike. Sage green banners replaced with darker charcoal pennants bearing a jagged fist emblem. Fantasy medieval European dark fortress style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Era 2 — Alanthor Culture Buildings

### Wall Hub
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A square fortified stone gatehouse that serves as a wall junction hub. A thick warm grey limestone tower with a wide arched passage through its center for units to pass. Crenellated battlements on top with space for defenders. Iron portcullis mechanism visible in the archway. Wall connection points extend from two or more sides where wall segments attach. A sage green banner hangs above the gate arch. Iron torch brackets on all four faces. Machicolations beneath the parapet for dropping projectiles. Murder holes visible in the archway ceiling. A robust, functional military fortification. Fantasy medieval European castle gatehouse style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Wall Segment
Isometric low-poly RTS building element. A straight section of thick fortified stone curtain wall. Warm grey limestone blocks with a crenellated parapet walkway wide enough for two defenders to patrol. Regular arrow-slit embrasures along the wall face. A reinforced base with a slight batter (angled thickening at the bottom). Iron torch brackets at intervals. The wall section has connection points at both ends for linking to wall hubs or other segments. Stone steps built into one side for defenders to climb up. Weathered but immaculately maintained masonry. Fantasy medieval European castle curtain wall style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Smelter
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A square industrial forge-smelter complex built from heavy warm grey limestone with iron structural reinforcements. Two large stone blast furnaces with tall chimney stacks billowing dark smoke and occasional sparks. An open-sided smithing area under a stone arch where molten metal glows orange in crucibles. Iron anvils, hammers, and tongs visible in the workspace. Rail tracks with a small ore cart leading to a raw materials stockpile area. Vents and grates glow with furnace heat along the base. Thick iron pipes connect the furnaces to quenching troughs. A sage green forgemaster's banner with an anvil-and-hammer emblem. The structure radiates industrial heat and purpose. Fantasy medieval European industrial forge style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Watch Tower
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A tall square stone watchtower for ranged defense. A slender but robust warm grey limestone tower rising three stories with a crenellated fighting platform at the top. Each level has arrow-slit windows on all four faces. The top platform has mounted crossbow emplacements and a brazier for fire signals and night illumination. A conical slate roof cap with an iron weathervane. The base is reinforced with a battered stone plinth. A single iron-banded door at ground level. Sage green pennant flying from the peak. Stone spiral staircase visible through an open window. The tower commands a wide field of view. Fantasy medieval European castle watchtower style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Garrison
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A large square fortified military compound for training elite soldiers. Massive warm grey limestone walls with prominent corner bastions and a crenellated parapet. A fortified entrance with double iron-studded gates beneath a carved stone arch bearing crossed swords. Inside the walls, a cobblestone drill yard is partially visible. A two-story main building with arched colonnades houses armories and quarters. Weapon and armor racks line the interior walls. A blacksmith's forge in one corner with an anvil and glowing coals. A tall square command tower in the rear corner flies a large sage green banner with a sentinel's shield emblem. Iron chain and plate armor pieces hang decoratively on the outer walls. Fantasy medieval European castle garrison style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Royal Stable
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A large square stone stable complex for training heavy cavalry. Warm grey limestone walls with a lower profile than military buildings but equally robust construction. A wide arched entrance big enough for mounted riders, with iron-shod double doors swung open. A row of stone-walled horse stalls visible inside with hay bales and feed troughs. An attached tack room with hanging saddles, barding, and lances visible through an arched window. A cobblestone paddock courtyard for mounting and training. A hayloft with a pulley hoist extends above the stalls. Water trough and hitching posts near the entrance. A sage green banner with a rearing horse emblem flies from a short pole. The stable conveys the wealth needed to maintain heavy warhorses. Fantasy medieval European castle stable style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Siege Yard
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A large square fortified workshop compound for constructing siege weapons. Heavy warm grey limestone walls surrounding an open-air construction yard. A wide gate with no doors for moving large siege equipment in and out. Inside, a partially assembled ballista sits on wooden supports. Stockpiles of heavy timber beams, iron bolts, rope coils, and iron fittings are organized in the yard. A roofed workshop section along one wall with workbenches, saws, and woodworking tools. A crane with a wooden boom and counterweight for lifting heavy components. Iron reinforcement plates lean against the walls. A forge corner for metalwork with bellows and an anvil. A sage green engineer's banner with a ballista emblem. Sawdust and wood shavings litter the ground. Fantasy medieval European castle siege workshop style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Style Notes

- **Stone color:** Warm grey limestone (#737068) as primary, darker grey (#555550) for foundations and accents
- **Fabric/banners:** Sage green (#8CA680) — Alanthor's primary culture color
- **Metal:** Dark iron and steel, occasionally with brass/gold trim on important buildings
- **Roofs:** Dark slate grey, steep pitched or flat crenellated depending on function
- **Shared motifs:** Crenellated battlements, arched doorways, iron reinforcements, square footprints, heraldic banners
- **Scale hierarchy:** Hall > Garrison/Stable/SiegeYard > Barracks/Smelter > Tower/GatherersHut > Hut
- **Lighting:** Warm interior glow from torches/forges, cool exterior stone
