# Feraldis Building Prompts

> **Culture Identity:** Bloodthirsty warband that wields arcane magic. Architecture is massive, angular, and oppressive — deep-carved stone fortresses with sharp angled features, countless spires, and dark arcane energy. Dwarven stone masonry meets dark elf underdark aesthetics. Colors: crimson red fabric/glowing runes, dark grey stone with obsidian accents.

---

## Era 1 — Universal Buildings (Feraldis Style)

### Hall
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A colossal angular stone stronghold serving as the faction's seat of power. Enormous blocks of dark grey granite fitted with brutal precision, walls angled inward like a dwarven mountain fortress. Sharp triangular buttresses jut from every face. A massive trapezoidal entrance carved deep into the rock with heavy iron-and-obsidian doors etched with arcane runes that glow faint crimson. Multiple jagged stone spires rise from the roofline at aggressive angles, their tips crackling with faint dark energy. Narrow slit windows glow with a deep red inner light. Iron chains with hanging braziers flank the entrance, burning with unnatural violet-red flame. Carved relief panels of war scenes and arcane sigils cover the lower walls. The stone itself has subtle dark purple mineral veining. Crimson banners bearing a fanged skull emblem hang from iron spikes. The structure radiates dread, dominance, and ancient power carved from the bones of the earth. Fantasy dwarven-underdark fortress style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Hut
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A squat angular stone dwelling hewn from dark grey granite blocks. Thick angled walls tapering slightly inward with sharp geometric edges — no curves anywhere. A heavy trapezoidal doorway with an iron-bound stone slab door carved with a single glowing crimson rune. One narrow triangular window slit glowing faintly red from within. A pointed stone spire rises from the peaked angular roof like a blade. Dark iron brackets hold a small brazier of violet-red flame beside the door. Obsidian accent stones set into the corners. The foundation stones are oversized, giving a sense of immovable weight. A small crimson pennant on an iron spike. Brutal, compact, and built to endure — even the humblest Feraldis dwelling looks like a bunker. Fantasy dwarven-underdark dwelling style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Gatherers Hut
Isometric low-poly RTS building. An angular dark stone storehouse and resource depot. Heavy dark grey granite walls with sharp angled buttresses and a steep angular roof. A wide trapezoidal entrance reinforced with iron framing, dark enough inside that faint crimson rune-light illuminates stacked resources — raw ore, dark crystals, bundled hides, and bone-carved crates. Iron hook racks on the exterior walls hold tools and harvesting implements. A stone conveyor ramp leads down to a lower receiving area. Two short pointed spires flank the roofline. Arcane preservation glyphs carved around the doorframe glow with a dim violet light, keeping stored goods from spoiling. Dark iron chains suspend a hanging sign with a claw-and-ore emblem. Obsidian capstones on the buttresses. Fantasy dwarven-underdark storehouse style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Barracks
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A towering angular stone barracks compound built for forging warriors. Massive dark grey granite walls rising at aggressive inward angles with sharp geometric crenellations like jagged teeth. A deep-set trapezoidal entrance flanked by carved stone gargoyle-like war effigies with glowing crimson eyes. An open training pit visible inside — blood-stained sand floor with weapon racks of brutal axes, serrated swords, and spiked maces lining the walls. Multiple pointed stone spires rise from the corners and ridgeline, the tallest bearing a crimson banner with crossed war-axes. Iron cage-like structures hang from the walls as trophies or punishment. Arcane runes carved along the parapet glow faint red, warding the structure. Braziers of violet-red flame mounted on angular iron stands light the entrance. Dark iron spikes line the rooftop edges. The building feels like it was carved from a mountain by something angry. Fantasy dwarven-underdark war barracks style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Era 1 — Choice Buildings (Feraldis Style)

### Temple of Ridan
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A towering angular cathedral-temple to the god Ridan, corrupted by Feraldis arcane tradition. Massive dark grey granite walls rising to sharp peaked gables at aggressive angles. A grand entrance formed by two angled stone pillars leaning inward to create a triangular portal, carved with dense arcane scripture that pulses with crimson light. A central spire — the tallest structure in the settlement — rises like a black blade from the apex, ringed with floating arcane sigils. Stained glass windows in deep crimson, violet, and black depict scenes of divine wrath and blood sacrifice. Smaller flanking spires cluster around the main tower like stone thorns. Interior candlelight flickers blood-red through the windows. Gargoyle sentinels perch at every corner, carved from obsidian. Heavy iron chains with ritual censers hang from the facade, trailing dark smoke. An atmosphere of terrifying reverence. Fantasy dwarven-underdark dark cathedral style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Vault of Almierra
Isometric low-poly RTS building. An immensely thick angular stone vault buried half into the ground. Dark grey granite blocks of enormous size locked together with obsidian clamps and iron brackets. No windows — only thin ventilation slits protected by iron grates. A single massive vault door of black iron set deep into an angular recess, covered in concentric rings of arcane locking glyphs that glow violet. Pointed defensive spires at each corner, shorter and thicker than other buildings — built to impale anyone scaling the walls. The angled walls lean inward creating a truncated pyramid shape. Carved ward-runes circle the entire structure at foundation level, glowing faint crimson as a theft deterrent. Dark iron chain barriers block the approach path. A crimson flag on each corner spire. The structure looks less like a building and more like a sealed dwarven tomb guarding something terrible within. Fantasy dwarven-underdark sealed vault style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Fiendstone Keep
Isometric low-poly RTS building. The Feraldis capital — a monstrous angular fortress of fiendstone radiating pure malevolence. Hewn from near-black fiendstone blocks laced with pulsing reddish-purple veins of arcane ore. Aggressively angled walls lean inward forming a truncated dark pyramid crowned with a forest of razor-sharp spires. The tallest central spire crackles with visible dark energy arcing between it and the surrounding lesser spires. A massive trapezoidal entrance flanked by two colossal carved stone war-demons with glowing ember eyes. Heavy portcullis of black iron with blade-like teeth. Every surface is carved with dense overlapping arcane sigils, battle scenes, and ward-patterns that glow and pulse in shifting crimson and violet. Chains of black iron hang from the upper walls bearing skulls and trophies. Murder holes and arrow slits glow with inner hellfire. Dark smoke and faint embers drift from vents in the spires. The ground around the foundation is cracked and scorched. Crimson banners with the jagged fist emblem hang torn and battle-worn. The keep feels alive with dark power. Fantasy dwarven-underdark dark citadel style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Era 2 — Feraldis Culture Buildings

### Hunting Lodge
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A broad angular stone lodge built for tracking and butchering prey. Dark grey granite walls with a steep angular roof of overlapping dark slate. A wide trapezoidal entrance hung with heavy hide curtains and animal pelts. Mounted beast skulls and antlers adorn the exterior walls as trophies — some unnaturally large, hinting at arcane prey. Drying racks of stretched hides and curing meat flank the building. A stone skinning table with iron tools sits near the entrance. A fire pit with a spit-roast smolders beside the lodge, burning with faintly violet flame. Pointed stone spires rise from the roof ridge. Hunting weapons — barbed spears, hooked nets, and bone-tipped javelins — hang from iron wall racks. Carved predator runes along the door frame glow faint crimson, blessing the hunt. Crimson pennant with a beast-skull emblem. The lodge smells of blood and smoke. Fantasy dwarven-underdark hunting hall style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Logging Station
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A heavy angular stone-and-iron lumber mill built with Feraldis brutality. Dark grey granite base walls supporting an open-sided upper structure with angular iron roof beams. Massive felled dark timber logs stacked in the yard, some petrified and laced with arcane mineral veins. A stone-mounted iron sawblade mechanism driven by a chain-and-gear system. Wood chips and sawdust cover the stone floor. Stacked planks and hewn beams organized against the walls. An anvil-and-forge station in one corner for crafting iron logging tools — axes, wedges, and chain hooks hanging from racks. Pointed stone spires at the two front corners. A heavy iron crane arm for moving large logs. Carved runes along the base stones glow faint crimson, enchanting the blades to cut harder. A crimson pennant with an axe-and-tree emblem. Smoke rises from the forge corner. Fantasy dwarven-underdark industrial lumber mill style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Longhouse
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A massive elongated angular stone war-hall for batch-training warriors. Dark grey granite walls stretching long and low with a steep angular ridged roof bristling with pointed stone spires along the entire spine. A grand trapezoidal entrance at the narrow end, wide enough for warriors to march out in formation, flanked by carved stone war-totems with glowing crimson eyes. Inside, the long hall is divided into training bays — visible through the entrance are racks of brutal weapons, armor stands, and sparring circles marked with arcane blood-runes on the stone floor. Iron chains hang from the ceiling rafters with suspended training dummies and weight blocks. Braziers of violet-red flame line the interior walls. A raised stone dais at the far end serves as the war-chief's command platform. The exterior walls bear carved relief murals of conquest and slaughter. Iron trophy hooks hold captured enemy banners. Crimson war-banner with a clenched fist emblem flies from the tallest central spire. The longhouse thrums with the energy of warriors being forged. Fantasy dwarven-underdark great war-hall style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Totem Tower
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A tall angular stone tower crowned with a massive arcane war-totem for ranged defense. A dark grey granite base — square, thick-walled, and angled inward — rises three stories to a fighting platform. The tower is topped with a large carved obsidian totem — a twisted effigy of stacked beast skulls and arcane sigils that crackles with visible dark energy between its points. The totem serves as both a weapon and a beacon, firing bolts of concentrated arcane force at enemies. Narrow angular arrow slits on every face glow faintly crimson. The fighting platform has angular stone merlons shaped like blades. Iron spike barriers ring the base as anti-siege defense. Carved ward-runes spiral up the exterior walls, glowing violet, channeling energy to the totem. A heavy iron door at the base with no handle — opened only by rune-command. Faint dark energy wisps drift upward from the totem crown. Crimson pennant lashed to an iron spike below the totem. Fantasy dwarven-underdark arcane defense tower style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

### Siege Yard
Isometric low-poly RTS building. A large angular stone compound for constructing brutal siege weapons. Dark grey granite perimeter walls with sharp angular buttresses and wide trapezoidal openings for moving massive equipment. A partially assembled siege ram dominates the yard — a colossal iron-headed battering ram on a heavy wheeled dark timber frame, its ram-head carved into a snarling beast face with glowing arcane runes along the shaft. Stockpiles of dark timber beams, iron plates, heavy chains, and obsidian-tipped bolts organized in the yard. A roofed forge section with multiple anvils, heavy hammers, and a large bellows-driven furnace glowing angry orange-red. A crane of dark iron and stone counterweight for lifting heavy siege components. Workbenches with war-machine blueprints pinned under iron weights. Pointed spires at each corner of the perimeter wall. Arcane-infused metalworking — some iron pieces glow with embedded runes. Crimson engineer's banner with a siege ram emblem. Sparks and dark smoke fill the air. Fantasy dwarven-underdark siege foundry style. Clean stylized RTS aesthetic, no background.

## Style Notes

- **Stone color:** Dark grey granite (#3A3A3D) as primary, near-black obsidian (#1A1A1E) for accents and carved elements
- **Fabric/banners:** Crimson red (#B32D1A) — Feraldis primary culture color
- **Glowing elements:** Crimson (#FF3322) for war-runes, violet (#8844AA) for arcane wards, ember-orange (#FF6622) for forges
- **Metal:** Black iron, dark steel, obsidian clamps and brackets
- **Roofs:** Dark slate, steep angular pitched, bristling with pointed stone spires
- **Shared motifs:** Sharp angled geometry (no curves), pointed stone spires, glowing arcane runes, trapezoidal doorways, obsidian accents, braziers of unnatural flame, carved war-reliefs
- **Scale hierarchy:** Fiendstone Keep > Hall/Longhouse > Barracks/Siege Yard > Hunting Lodge/Logging Station > Gatherers Hut > Hut
- **Lighting:** Deep crimson/violet arcane glow from within, angry orange forge-light, violet-red brazier flame
- **Key distinction from Alanthor:** Where Alanthor is square and refined, Feraldis is angular and aggressive. Alanthor has arches; Feraldis has trapezoidal openings. Alanthor has flat crenellations; Feraldis has blade-like merlons and spires everywhere.
